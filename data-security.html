Data Security & Compliance

Designed to remove procurement friction

Data handling practices

  • Access limited to project scope and need-to-know
  • Secure storage during analysis
  • Outputs shared through approved channels

We can align to your municipality’s preferred transfer and retention requirements.

Secure transfer methods

  • Secure file transfer (SFTP or equivalent)
  • Encrypted archives when needed
  • Municipality-approved sharing options
Goal: Use what your clerk/IT already trusts, not a new workflow.

HIPAA awareness

Many public safety datasets may include sensitive fields. While you may not be a covered entity, we handle data with appropriate care and minimize use of sensitive fields where possible.

  • Minimize exposure of sensitive identifiers
  • Aggregate reporting where feasible
  • Redaction for board/public-facing outputs

Retention & deletion

We can retain data only as long as needed for the engagement and delete upon completion, subject to any municipality requirements.

  • Defined retention window
  • Deletion confirmation available upon request
  • Project-specific scope control
